FEEDING MIAMI TOGETHER

From June 1st to July 20th, One More Child Miami campus distributed 300,948 meals, meaning 361,138 pounds of food, across the entire South Florida region of Miami Dade, Broward, and Monroe County. Food was distributed in partnership with 36 churches, 16 Lions Club International centers, and 10 Greater Federation Woman's Clubs.

Through a partnership with Little Caesars, the Miami campus distributed 402 pizzas to 21 partners in Miami Dade county over a two week period. Partners included volunteer groups, foster group homes, homeless shelters, police stations, nursing homes, and hospitals.

Through two special distributions with the Florida Department of Children and Families and Citrus Family Network, we distributed to over 300 families that are in the foster care, group medical homes, special needs family, and emotional/behavioral support homes. We had 809 volunteers serve the community with a total number of 58,132 people impacted.
